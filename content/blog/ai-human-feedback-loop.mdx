# The AI-Human Feedback Loop: A First Look at `gitlegend://` URLs

The `gitlegend://` URL is a core innovation in GitLegend. It acts as a bridge, allowing a user to pass specific historical context from the dashboard directly to their AI assistant, and vice-versa. This creates a powerful feedback loop between human developers and their AI partners.
