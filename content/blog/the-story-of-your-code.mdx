# The Story of Your Code: Why We Built GitLegend

We believe that every codebase has a story to tell. A story of collaboration, of innovation, and of evolution. But too often, that story is locked away in the git history, difficult to access and understand. That's why we built GitLegend, a platform to understand and narrate the history of a software project.
